About MKSU Hackfest

MKSU Hackfest is an annual event that brings together talented developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts from around the world for a weekend of coding, collaboration, and creativity. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting out, this hackfest is the perfect place to unleash your skills, learn from others, and build something amazing.

During the event, you'll have the opportunity to participate in challenges, attend workshops and talks, network with industry professionals, and showcase your projects to a panel of expert judges. With plenty of prizes and swag up for grabs, MKSU Hackfest is an unforgettable experience that you won't want to miss.
